How do I get a key to the Engineering Building?

Access to the Engineering Building beyond normal operating hours is granted via swipe card. Graduate students are not given after-hours access to the Engineering Building by default, but students can acquire it if needed. To activate your MSU ID for access to the Engineering Building, follow these steps. First, explain to your faculty advisor why you require access. Then, your adviser will notify Norma Teague of your need. Norma will, in turn, process your request to have access to the Engineering Building added to your ID. To expedite your request, please have your advisor include your PID and proxy number (the number following the * on the back of your MSU ID) with the request.

Access to labs and offices is by key. You may request a key to your lab or office through your advisor as well. The process is the same. You will make a request to your advisor who will then submit the request to Norma Teague, who will in turn issue you a key. Finally, pick up your key from Norma in the CSE office. You must return your key to Norma prior to graduation, or the department will place a hold on your degree. Please direct any questions you have regarding building keys to Kayra Hopkins.

How do I access on-line resources like the ACM Digital Library and IEEE Xplore when I'm off campus?

You can access these resources using the library's proxy server. The easiest way to use the proxy is to simply search for materials via the MSU Library's Electronic Resources page. If you use a firewall, you will need to allow outgoing connections on ports 2047 and 443.
