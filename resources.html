Labs and Lab Liaisons

To better maintain open lines of communication with grads in the various CSE research groups, the CSEGA recognizes a volunteer from each lab as a liaison. They also ensure that the interests of their respective research groups are represented.

If your CSE lab or research group is not represented and would like to supply a liaison to the CSEGA, please, send a request naming the group and the grad student who will act as liaison to csega(at)cse.msu.edu.

Student Represenatives

Each Fall, CSE graduate students elect representatives for several important intra- and extradepartmental functions.
